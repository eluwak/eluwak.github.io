## 75‑word company synopsis & why RT

Automated Test Equipment racks can be very expensive, and are sensitive to various factors that could
cause catastrophic harm to the rack, Units Under Test (UUTs), or even operating personnel. This is the
reason why a Rack Environment Monitor is needed that can monitor and control activities in a predictable
manner. Specific and deterministic behavior in unexpected scenarios could help save the company from
undesired expenses related to damaged hardware or injured personnel.
